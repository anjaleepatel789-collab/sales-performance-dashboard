# Sales Performance Dashboard — Excel

## Project Overview
End-to-end Excel project on 1,500 sales records — from raw messy data
to a fully interactive business dashboard with actionable insights.

**Tools used:** Microsoft Excel — Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers,
Data Cleaning, KPI Card Design, Dashboard Layout

## What I Did — Step by Step

### 1. Data Cleaning (Raw_Data → Cleaned_Data)
- Started with 1,500 rows and 19 columns of raw sales data
- Added Month and Year columns for time-based analysis
- Calculated Revenue column from Quantity × UnitPrice
- Handled 370 missing values in the Promotion column
- Standardized date fields and removed formatting inconsistencies

### 2. Analysis Sheets Built
- **Product_Analysis** — revenue % share by product using Pivot Tables
- **Region_Analysis** — revenue % share across 5 regions
- **Product_Region_Analysis** — cross-tab of product × region revenue breakdown
- **Monthly_Analysis** — monthly revenue trend across 2023, 2024, and 2025

### 3. Dashboard Features
- 4 KPI Cards: Total Revenue · Total Orders · Top Product · Top Region
- Revenue by Product — bar chart comparing all 7 categories
- Revenue by Region — bar chart across 5 regions
- Monthly Revenue Trend — line chart comparing 2023, 2024, 2025
- Dynamic Slicers — filter everything by Product, Region, and Year

## Key Business Insights

| # | Insight |
|---|---------|
| 1 | Printer, Laptop, and Tablet are near-equal top contributors (~15.6% each) |
| 2 | North region leads with 22.2% of total revenue |
| 3 | Phone is the weakest product — only 11.3% revenue share |
| 4 | Revenue grew from $1.83M (2023) to $1.91M (2024) — 4.5% YoY growth |
| 5 | 24.8% return rate across 1,500 orders — a key business risk flagged |
| 6 | Average order delivery time: 6 days (range: 1–10 days) |

## Workbook Structure (7 Sheets)

| Sheet | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| Raw_Data | Original 1,500 records as received |
| Cleaned_Data | Cleaned version with added Month, Year, Revenue columns |
| Product_Analysis | Pivot — revenue % by product |
| Region_Analysis | Pivot — revenue % by region |
| Product_Region_Analysis | Cross-tab — product × region revenue |
| Monthly_Analysis | Pivot — monthly revenue by year |
| Dashboard | Final interactive dashboard |
